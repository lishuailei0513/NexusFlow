"The Strategy Gap": The Problem in the Market

Why 90% of traders still lose money on the best platforms.

Platforms Are Just Tools

TradingView and MT5 are powerful "kitchens," but they don't provide "Michelin-star recipes." Users still need to find or develop their own strategies.

Failure of Emotion & Discipline

During manual execution, traders struggle to overcome emotional decisions and poor risk management, causing strategies to fail.

Crisis of Trust

The MQL5 marketplace is flooded with non-transparent, over-fitted "black boxes" that are hard to trust.

The Solution: A SaaS Layer Built on "Trust" and "Technology"

We don't build platforms; we make your existing platforms smarter.

1. Validated Strategy Library (SaaS Subscription)

We provide a library of rigorously validated and transparently backtested quantitative strategies (as EAs/indicators/scripts), 100% compatible with MT5 and TradingView. We don't sell "black boxes"; we offer professional, fully transparent, and verifiable tools.

2. Smart Trading Assistant (SaaS Subscription)

A dashboard-style EA/plugin. It's **not** a strategy itself but a powerful assistant that helps traders **execute any strategy** better. Core features include automated risk calculation, one-click order execution, multi-position management, and auto break-even, all designed to bring discipline and efficiency.

3. Quant Research & Customization (Service)

Leveraging the founder's 10+ years of professional software engineering experience, we offer "strategy-to-code" custom development services for advanced clients. This provides crucial initial cash flow and helps us gain deep insights into the needs of power users.
